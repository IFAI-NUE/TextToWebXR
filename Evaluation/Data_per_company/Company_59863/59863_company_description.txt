About Us: DMG MORI is a global leader in manufacturing high-precision machine tools and sustainable technologies that are at the beginning of worldwide value chains. Our core business of turning and milling machines, Advanced Technologies, and Additive Manufacturing is enhanced by comprehensive automation and seamless digitalization solutions. We focus our technological excellence on key industries such as Aerospace, Automotive, Die & Mold, Medical, and Semiconductor.

As a "Global One Company," approximately 12,000 employees engage directly with over 100,000 customers from 55 industries. DMG MORI operates globally in 87 countries, with 16 production plants and 111 sales and service locations, actively advancing the future fields of automation, digitalization, and sustainability.
