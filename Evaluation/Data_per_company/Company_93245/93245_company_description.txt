About Us: As a leading construction and real estate service provider, our company develops, realizes, and manages living spaces, workplaces, and infrastructure for future generations in Switzerland and Germany. Additionally, we offer tunneling and related infrastructure projects in other markets. Founded in 2006, the company draws on approximately 150 years of construction tradition. It integrates expertise from highly skilled consulting, development, planning, and execution units under one roof, forming a leading multinational construction and real estate service provider. With a broad range of services and specialist expertise, the group handles large, complex projects and supports clients throughout the entire lifecycle of their structures. The focus is on customer needs and maintaining a sustainable balance between economic success and social and environmental responsibility. Headquartered near Zurich, the company employs around 9,000 people across Europe and achieved a revenue of CHF 3.6 billion in 2022.
