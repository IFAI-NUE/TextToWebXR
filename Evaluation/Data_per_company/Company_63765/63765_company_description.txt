About Us: Our company, a subsidiary of a renowned engineering group, is internationally recognized for innovation and excellence in developing, producing, and distributing advanced solutions for nanotechnology applications in both industry and research. Our portfolio includes high-precision nano drives, sensors, and microscope systems that operate at the limits of what is physically and technically possible. All products are developed and manufactured at our headquarters near Munich. An international team of physicists, engineers, software developers, and product designers collaborates closely from concept to delivery. We also have a sales office in the USA and an additional location in Europe. The company has won various significant awards, including a prestigious innovation award and a national entrepreneur prize.
