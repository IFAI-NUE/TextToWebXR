About Us: WIKUTEC is one of the leading mid-sized family businesses in Upper Franconia, specializing in plastics and injection molding. Founded in 2001, the company produces high-quality plastic components for various applications and industries. Besides the toy industry, WIKUTEC's products are primarily used in the automotive sector. High-quality plastic components "Made in Selbitz" are found in vehicles from brands like Audi, BMW, Mercedes, Porsche, and VW. WIKUTEC is an innovative partner from development to mass production.
