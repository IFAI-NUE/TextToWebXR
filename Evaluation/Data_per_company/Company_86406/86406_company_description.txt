About Us: Based in the Nuremberg metropolitan area with additional locations in Hersbruck, Pfaffenhofen an der Ilm, and Stuttgart, Eiffage Infra-Süd primarily executes projects in road construction, civil engineering, and rail and track construction. The company is also a reliable partner for major projects. Eiffage Infra-Süd consolidates all traditional construction services under one roof and implements them with a high degree of in-house production. The company is part of Eiffage Infra-Bau.
