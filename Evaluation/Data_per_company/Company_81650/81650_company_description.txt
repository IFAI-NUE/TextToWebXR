About Us: IBC SOLAR is a leading full-service provider of energy solutions and services in the field of photovoltaics and storage. The company offers complete systems covering the entire spectrum from planning to turnkey delivery of photovoltaic systems. The offerings include energy solutions for homes, businesses, and industries, as well as solar parks. In this area, IBC SOLAR acts as a project developer and general contractor, planning, executing, and marketing large solar projects worldwide. IBC SOLAR collaborates with an extensive network of expert partners to ensure competent and high-quality system installations globally. Founded in 1982 in Bad Staffelstein, the company is now active in over 30 countries.
