About Us: Hello! We are Kaweco, and we've been crafting quality writing instruments since 1883. Our pens stand for quality and design, meticulously created with attention to detail using modern materials and colors. The brand is now managed by a company based in Nuremberg, Langwasser, and continues to gain momentum. We are steadily expanding internationally and are now present in over 70 countries.

Teamwork is a major focus for us. In our dynamic and humorous team, you can leverage your strengths to transform the world of writing instruments with the storied Kaweco brand. We provide you with the trust you need to contribute to this success. Our company structure offers the necessary flexibility and creative freedom.
