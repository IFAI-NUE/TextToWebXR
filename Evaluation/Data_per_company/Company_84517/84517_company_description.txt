About Us: We are an internationally active manufacturing and wholesale company based in two locations in Bavaria, Germany. We offer a uniquely broad and efficient range of accessories for animal breeding and husbandry, constantly updated to reflect current trends across Europe. This includes products for livestock and agriculture needs, equestrian supplies, fencing, work and safety gear, hobby farming, and pet supplies. Currently, we employ 730 people across our locations in Germany, Austria, France, Poland, and the United Kingdom.
