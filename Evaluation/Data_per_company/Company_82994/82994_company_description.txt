About Us: For around 130 years, we've exemplified what others can't replicate: providing up to 80,000 people in the region daily with 100% green electricity and gas, water, district heating, and related services. With a pioneering spirit, we are committed to implementing the local energy transition by 2035 and focusing on climate protection. Through our activities, we stimulate the region's prosperity and promote sustainable progress.

Along with our AmperOase, a versatile recreational facility featuring indoor and outdoor pools, a sauna area, and an ice rink, we contribute to the community's valued quality of life. We take pride in supporting the growth and high living standards of the region.
