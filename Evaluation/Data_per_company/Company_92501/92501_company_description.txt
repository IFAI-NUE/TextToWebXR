About Us: For over 45 years, our company has been a medium-sized energy provider specializing in the supply of electricity, natural gas, and energy services for both private and business customers. 

Our team of 40 operates from our headquarters in Aschaffenburg and a branch in Sailauf. We offer guidance on everything from natural gas connections to the finalization of energy supply contracts. As a forward-thinking company, we have long recognized the potential of innovative topics, incorporating electromobility and renewable energy into our portfolio, and providing various solutions in the expanding field of energy services.
