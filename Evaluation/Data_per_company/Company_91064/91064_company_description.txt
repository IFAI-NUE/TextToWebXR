About Us: We are a modern service company in the public sector, supporting students in the region. With around 300 employees, we assist, support, and provide services to 70,000 students at eleven universities in the area. We operate 25 dining halls and other campusdining facilities, manage 21 housing complexes with 3,600 spaces, oversee the office for educational funding, and run 5 childcare centers for students' children. Additionally, we offer a wide range of advisory and cultural services for students.
