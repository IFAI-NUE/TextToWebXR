About Us: Intelligent Systems and Solutions for Tomorrow's Innovation.

Our company is a globally operating enterprise focused on switchgear and control panel construction, energy and building technology, as well as the production of vehicle cabins, e-containers, and enclosures. Since our founding in 1995, we have prioritized impeccable quality in our products and services. In a short time, we have become a trusted partner for internationally leading clients due to our high-quality and creative solutions. Our specialized expertise allows our products to be used in various applications worldwide.

With certifications for both national and international markets, we meet the highest quality standards and strive for continuous improvement of our processes and services. We value open communication, satisfied employees, and modern, future-oriented development and production.
