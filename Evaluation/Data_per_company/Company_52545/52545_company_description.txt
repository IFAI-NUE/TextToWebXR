About Us: We develop and manufacture high-precision, innovative components and systems for the future of automobiles. Our family-owned company has been a leading development and system partner in the industry for many years, supplying major international car manufacturers and suppliers. With around 4,000 employees at twelve locations, we generated over 500 million euros in revenue last year. We operate across three continents in six countries: the USA, Mexico, Germany, the Czech Republic, Romania, and China. Our locations in Germany include Wiggensbach/Allgäu, Schorndorf/Stuttgart, Fürth, and Karlsruhe.
