About Us: Since 1978, our company has been renowned for comprehensive customer care, craftsmanship, and creativity in high-quality interior construction for both private and commercial projects, even beyond regional borders. Our philosophy is to blend traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. We offer not only solid expertise and decades of experience but also computer-controlled production with the latest CNC technology. With our innovative and modern machinery, we are well-equipped to provide high-end furniture and interior construction services from a single source. 

The foundation of our work and the quality we stand for is our team, which is well-trained and highly motivated to meet our customers' needs. Our employees are part of a coordinated and dedicated team that skillfully handles everything from planning and design to manufacturing and professional installation with utmost precision, enjoying the craft in the era of Industry 4.0.
