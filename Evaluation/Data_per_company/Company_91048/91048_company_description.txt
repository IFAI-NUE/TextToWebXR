About Us: The BMZ Group is a global leader in producing lithium-ion battery system solutions. With its headquarters in Germany, the group operates production facilities in China, Poland, and the USA, and has offices in Japan and France. Additionally, there are research and development sites worldwide. BMZ GmbH boasts 25 years of experience and has been recognized for its growth strength, receiving the "Bavaria's Best 50" award for the fourth time. In 2018, BMZ was honored as a future-oriented employer with the "IKOM Award" from a prominent university in Munich. Approximately 3,000 employees work for the BMZ Group globally.
