About Us: With a history spanning over 230 years, our group focuses on innovation, adaptability, and sustainability. Through our segments in specialized foundation engineering, machinery, and resources, we provide solutions for global challenges, supported by our 12,000 employees and the trust of our clients.

In construction, we offer both traditional and new, innovative foundation engineering methods, executing foundations, excavations, diaphragm walls, and ground improvements worldwide.

In machinery, we are a global leader offering a full range of equipment for specialized foundation engineering, as well as exploration, development, and extraction of natural resources.

In the resources segment, we develop innovative products in drilling services and well construction, environmental technology, plant engineering, mining, and remediation.

With numerous subsidiaries, our group has a global network of offices and production sites from Houston to Singapore, and from Hamburg to Johannesburg.
