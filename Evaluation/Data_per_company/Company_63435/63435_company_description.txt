About Us: Our motto at BAYERNOIL is "Success Together." This means we put our approximately 850 employees at the center of everything we do. Our success is made possible by their efforts. BAYERNOIL is located in the heart of Bavaria, with our two refinery sites in Vohburg and Neustadt, nestled between Ingolstadt and Regensburg. Our region is among the most beautiful in Germany. You'll find an excellent educational infrastructure and countless opportunities for culture and sports enthusiasts, from classical music festivals to Bundesliga football. Here, you can live, work, learn, and enjoy life to the fullest.
