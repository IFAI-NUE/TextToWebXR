About Us: Efficient. Eco-conscious. Sustainable. For over 60 years, we’ve been doing what everyone is talking about today. Whether it’s helping to produce biofuels, process fish by-products, or recycle plastics, our machines contribute to truly sustainable operations.

We are a family-owned company specializing in separation technology. Our goal is to achieve tangible success for our clients. Our team understands what users need, enabling us to guarantee greater success with the right solutions.

Our machines are exclusively manufactured in Vilsbiburg, Bavaria. We emphasize a high degree of added value, from machining and assembly to completion in plant construction. During delivery and commissioning, our specialists are on-site with clients to ensure 100% functionality and satisfaction.
