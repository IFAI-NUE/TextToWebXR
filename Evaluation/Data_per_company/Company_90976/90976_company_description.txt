About Us: Our products and solutions aim to inspire people, enhance quality of life, and contribute to conserving natural resources. In short, our mission is TECHNOLOGY FOR LIFE.

Since 1957, our company has had a manufacturing site in Ansbach. Our diverse product portfolio includes a wide range of electronic control units and mechatronic components for the automotive industry. We are part of the Mobility Electronics (ME) division and take on comprehensive tasks within the global manufacturing network. With around 2,500 employees, our company is one of the largest industrial employers in the West-Middle Franconia region. Staff from 28 nations work at the Ansbach plant.

Our group is a leading international technology and service company, with activities divided into four business sectors. Overarching trends such as automation, electrification, digitalization, connectivity, and a focus on sustainability are increasingly shaping the group's operations.
