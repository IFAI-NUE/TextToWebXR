About Us: Our company specializes in designing and constructing halls, residential buildings, stables, and equestrian facilities, with branches in Austria and Switzerland. We focus on agricultural, commercial, and residential construction, equipping roofs with photovoltaic systems. As a family-run enterprise with over 60 years of experience, we offer comprehensive services: from 3D visual planning and in-house production at our energy-efficient location to assembly. With approximately 750 employees, we complete around 600 buildings annually, emphasizing innovation and high quality.
