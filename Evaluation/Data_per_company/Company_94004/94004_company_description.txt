About us: KYOCERA AVX is a global leader in advanced electronic components, aimed at accelerating technological innovations and creating a better future. As a wholly owned subsidiary of Kyocera Corporation, KYOCERA AVX boasts an extensive global presence with research, development, and production facilities in over 15 countries.

KYOCERA AVX products support a variety of eco-friendly technologies designed to conserve existing energy resources and create reliable systems. Their components are found in countless products that help ensure both current and future generations benefit from clean, environmentally friendly technologies.

At our location in Neuhaus-Schierschnitz, we currently employ around 180 staff members in automation, component manufacturing, and hydrogen technology.
