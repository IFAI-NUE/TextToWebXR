About Us: Our company is a global family-owned industrial enterprise. Our corporate culture is deeply rooted in tradition, sustainability, and mutual respect. With approximately 15,000 employees worldwide, and over 3,300 colleagues at our headquarters, we are a sought-after employer in the region. We are a leading global manufacturer of both standardized and custom connection solutions in high-frequency, high-voltage, and fiber-optic technology. Renowned high-tech companies in mobile and telecommunications, data technology, medical electronics, industrial measurement, and automotive electronics rely on the precision and quality of our products. Our machinery division is well-regarded as a supplier of components for technically demanding devices and systems.
