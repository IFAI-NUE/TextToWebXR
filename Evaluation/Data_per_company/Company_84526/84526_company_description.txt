About Us: Our owner-managed group, with headquarters in Waldkraiburg, boasts over 75 years of successful history. With more than 420 employees worldwide, we are among the market leaders in fully automatic food weighing. 

Our success is built on the high-quality and technical standards of our multihead weighers. Development and production take place exclusively at our headquarters in Waldkraiburg. In addition to a global network of representatives, our products are distributed through four subsidiaries located in the UK, Netherlands, France, and the USA.

Our extensive expertise and consistently stable order situation guarantee long-term success.
