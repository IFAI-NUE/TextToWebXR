About Us: Since our founding in 1926, we have been a global family-owned company, leading the way in developing and manufacturing highly innovative production and automation systems. Our clients include renowned car manufacturers and their suppliers, as well as companies from various industries. As a family business, we focus on quality, precision, and sustainable growth from our own resources. With production facilities in Germany, China, USA, Brazil, Italy, and India, along with service and sales offices worldwide, we employ over 9,000 people globally, ensuring the highest quality.
