About Us: Established in 1999, PROBAT Bau AG is now a leading construction company in Bavaria, with headquarters in Feldkirchen near Munich and a branch in Ingolstadt. We work with private individuals, investors, companies, and public institutions on renovation and new construction projects. Our roles range from general contractor to specialist in existing structures, new builds, turnkey projects, drywall, and construction elements.

With approximately 300 employees, we prioritize a high percentage of permanent staff and long-term trade partners. PROBAT aims to be a top employer, offering security, development opportunities, and an exciting future outlook for its employees. This dedication translates into exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.
