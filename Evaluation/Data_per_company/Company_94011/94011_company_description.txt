About Us: Our organization is officially recognized across all regions of Germany for overseeing vehicle safety. Independent automotive experts and their staff work nationwide under our mandate.

Since 2022, our engineering firm located in the Coburg area has been providing services related to vehicle safety.

Our services include:

- Vehicle Inspections (HU): Regular technical inspections mandated by law. New vehicles are inspected three years after initial registration and then every two years.

- Emissions Testing (AU): Part of our environmental protection efforts, these tests aim for early fault detection and fuel consumption reduction. Since January 1, 2010, emissions testing is included as part of the main inspection.

- Safety checks, modification approvals, classic car appraisals, and gas inspections for RVs and caravans.

- Damage Assessments: We provide unbiased damage reports to support insurance claims.
