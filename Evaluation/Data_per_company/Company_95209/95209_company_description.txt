About Us: Our group includes SCALTEL GmbH & Co. KG, SCALTEL Smart Building GmbH, SCALCOM GmbH, and SCALTEL SNS Systems GmbH. The SCALTEL Group is among the leading Managed Service Providers in Germany. With a comprehensive product and service portfolio, it meets the needs of businesses in IT infrastructure and IT security. Approximately 300 employees contribute to the group's success. Based out of locations in Waltenhofen, Wiesbaden, and Neuss, the SCALTEL team serves medium-sized businesses, public clients, and companies in the healthcare sector.
