About Us: The Bavarian Cooperative Association represents the interests of Bavarian cooperatives for over 130 years. With around 500 employees, we are among the larger auditing organizations in Germany. Our membership includes more than 1,200 institutions such as banks and companies from sectors like agriculture, energy, commerce, crafts, and services. They form one of the largest medium-sized business organizations in the region, with approximately 50,000 employees and 2.9 million stakeholders.
