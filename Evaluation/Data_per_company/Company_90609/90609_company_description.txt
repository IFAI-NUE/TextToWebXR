About us: Founded in 1925 as a traditional construction company, our organization is now a third-generation, medium-sized family business. Our range of services includes not only residential and commercial construction, but also turnkey projects, project development, and a nationwide milling operation equipped for all tasks.

Our headquarters is located in Memmingen. In addition to this, we maintain several branches in various regions, employing around 1,000 people. The wider group includes numerous subsidiaries spread across Germany, handling downstream construction processes.

The group employs a total of approximately 2,300 staff members. Quality, flexibility, and reliability have always been our core values, embraced daily. Highly trained employees and a modern fleet of machinery ensure that our services are sustainable, economical, and professional.

This guarantees, for both us and our clients, that all requirements are met responsibly, economically, and efficiently.
