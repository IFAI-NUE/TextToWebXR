About Us: We are a global company offering high-quality and cost-effective solutions for the fine machining of gears and profiles. With around 1,000 employees, we embody innovation and the expertise developed over 120 years as a sustainably managed family business.

With production sites in Germany and sales and service branches worldwide, we are present in all major markets quickly and reliably. We partner with companies from various industries, including mobility, automation, and energy.

The perfect synergy of machines, tools, and technology enables precise machining to thousandths of a millimeter and up to a diameter of eight meters. Each system solution is individually optimized to meet customer requirements and supported throughout its entire lifecycle. Highly accurate measurement technology complements our comprehensive product portfolio to ensure the required quality.

We enhance efficient and stable production processes with innovative services and digital solutions.
