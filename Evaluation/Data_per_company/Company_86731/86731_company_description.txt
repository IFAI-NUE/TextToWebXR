About Us: Founded in 1974, our company is a leading manufacturer of carton end-packaging machines. For 50 years, we have been shaping the future of packaging technology through the development and construction of innovative packaging systems. With our headquarters in Amerang, manufacturing facility in Haag, and additional locations in the USA and Thailand, we operate globally. As a technology leader in this field, we excel through the perfect combination of extensive experience, innovation, and a highly skilled team. Our commitment to excellence and passion for high-tech are the foundations of our success. With over 500 dedicated employees across four locations and continuous growth, we take pride in shaping the packaging technology of tomorrow. Our family business fosters a modern corporate culture centered on openness and unity. We provide innovative, tailored packaging solutions worldwide for renowned producers in the food, pet food, and non-food sectors.
