About Us: Agricultural Association

Over 450 staff members support our 135,000 member farms in Bavaria. The Agricultural Association is a trusted partner and strong representative for farming families in Bavaria, as well as a responsible employer for its employees.

Career and Personal Advancement Opportunities

In your role at the Agricultural Association, you will assist Bavarian farming families in developing solutions for the challenges of modern agriculture. You will handle diverse tasks that are closely related to agricultural practice and rural life.

Join the team with your expertise. Pursue a meaningful career in an industry with a promising future.

Find more information on our career page.

Compensation

As an employee of the Agricultural Association, you enjoy the benefits and conditions of the public service tariff agreement of the federal states. We offer flexibility, diverse opportunities across the entire organization, and career possibilities in both full-time and part-time roles. We also provide a company pension to plan for your future.
