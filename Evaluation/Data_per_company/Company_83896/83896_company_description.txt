About Us: The institute at the Faculty of Social Sciences at the Technical University of Nuremberg consolidates projects, expert knowledge, and activities related to electronically mediated communication in the psychosocial counseling field. The institute researches trends, impact, and quality of online counseling, coaches social organizations in implementing online counseling, and offers a broad qualification program. Every September, the institute hosts the Online Counseling Forum, where professionals discuss current developments in the sector. As part of digitalization, online counseling is a crucial component for providing accessible and forward-thinking support for people in specific life situations.
